About Talentship Global Advisory Forum

Empowering Talents. Enabling Transformation. Envisioning Universal Prosperity.

Unlocking Potential, Empowering Communities

At Talentship Global Advisory Forum, we believe that every individual possesses unique talents, and every venture holds untapped potential. We are a nonprofit startup dedicated to harnessing human capabilities and utilizing them for the greater good of society. Our mission is to empower individuals, organizations, and communities by providing expert advisory services, fostering collaboration, and envisioning a future where everyone can thrive.

Pro Bono Consulting for Social Impact

Talentship Global Advisory Forum is committed to making a positive difference by offering free consulting and advisory services to small businesses, startups, local businesses, shops, and small restaurants across diverse domains. Our focus is on social impact and community empowerment. Through our expertise, we assist these ventures in overcoming challenges, identifying growth opportunities, and developing sustainable strategies. We firmly believe that supporting the backbone of our economy leads to a stronger society overall. One of our key objectives is to offer work experience opportunities to undergraduate students, specifically those who are aspiring to pursue postgraduate studies such as MTech, MBA, or Masters. We understand the importance of work experience in today's competitive world and how it plays a crucial role in securing admission to prestigious postgrad colleges. Join our NGO, work on exciting projects aligned with your interests, and dedicate just 1-2 hours per day. But wait, it gets even better!

You can showcase your work experience with prestigious validation! Present an appointment letter, experience letter, and recommendation letter meticulously researched to meet CAT/GMAT/GATE/UPSC requirements.
